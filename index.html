00    Mission
We invest in technology that gives power back to people.

Simple People Holdings backs ventures built on data ownership, transparency, and simplicity so that everyone can benefit from the digital world.

Your data belongs to you. We invest in the companies making that a reality. Every product under our roof is held to the same standard: honest by design, built for the people who use it.

We operate openly. Every dollar we earn reinvests back into the communities we serve, not shareholders, not advertisers.
